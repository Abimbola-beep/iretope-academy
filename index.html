About Iretope Academy

Iretope Academy offers quality Creche, Nursery, Primary, and Secondary education in Ibadan. Our dedicated team of qualified teachers ensures that every child thrives academically, socially, and morally.

With modern facilities, a well-equipped playground, laboratory, and vocational studies, we are committed to shaping future leaders through excellence in education.
